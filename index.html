About


Dear Valued Guest, First and foremost thank you for choosing order with us. We sincerely hope that your experience here always be an enjoyable one! Since 2006, the family and staff of China Fan have cherished each and every opportunity to serve you, your family, and your friends. Thanks for making us one of your neighborhood favorites! We have had many successful years thus far and can only hope to continue to serve great food to our community. We are very pleased to announce that we will be able to offer more than ever before! Now we have a much more dynamic menu with the addition of a brand new state of art sushi bar, custom fitted kitchen and casual atmosphere dining room, A lot of time, effort, and thought went into revamping our look and menu. Everything in house, fresh and "a la minute" just as you would in your home. Furthermore we remain committed to serve dishes with only the freshest ingredients available. After two generations of restaurant and culinary experience we are not only proud of our safeguarded family recipes and expertise but also of the hard earned relationships formed with New York and New Jersey's finest suppliers. After all, great tasting food starts with great ingredients. We have toiled with countless numbers of suppliers to guarantee that we receive only the very best cuts of meat, the freshest fish, and the most beautiful produce around. We wouldn't give our family, or yours, anything less. Please feel free to leave any suggestions or comments with us. Your opinions, whether compliments or criticisms, will help us in our mission to constantly improve.
